```
[Food/beverage product] professional food photography,

natural daylight aesthetic, [surface/props],

```
*   **Freshness cues:** Steam for warmth, condensation for cold, fresh herbs/garnishes.
*   **Lighting:** Backlit for steam, side lighting for texture, warm tones for appetite.

---

## Lighting Mastery
*   **Three-Point (Classic):** Key (main), Fill (soften shadows), Rim (edge definition).
*   **Window Light:** Soft diffused daylight, organic shadows, lifestyle aesthetic.
*   **Golden Hour:** Warm directional light, long soft shadows.

---

## Background & Staging
*   **Pure White (#FFFFFF):** Best for Amazon/E-Commerce.
*   **Light Gray Gradient:** Best for Website Hero sections.
*   **Surface Staging:** Use Marble (Luxury), Wood (Warm/Organic), Linen (Lifestyle).

---

## Platform-Specific Optimization
*   **Amazon:** Pure white background, 85% frame fill.
*   **Instagram:** Lifestyle context, 1:1 or 4:5 aspect ratio, aesthetic storytelling.
*   **Hero Banner:** Negative space left/right for text overlay.

---

## Quality Checklist
1. **Technical Quality:** Sharp focus, no AI artifacts?
2. **Product Accuracy:** Colors and shapes consistent with brand?
3. **Commercial Quality:** Does it look like a pro shot or an "AI prompt"?
4. **Platform Fit:** Does it meet the target platform's requirements?
