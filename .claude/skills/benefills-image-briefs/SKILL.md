### Typography & Text in Images

**Primary Font:** Clean sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins, or similar)
**Secondary Font:** Friendly script (for accents only, e.g., "ThyroVibe")

**Text Hierarchy:**
- **Headlines:** Bold, 60-80pt, deep navy or teal
- **Subheads:** Medium weight, 40-50pt
- **Body Text:** Regular, 24-32pt (must be readable on mobile)

**Layout Rules:**
- Left-align or center-align (avoid right-align for readability)
- Generous white spaceâ€”never crowded
- Text should contrast strongly with background (WCAG AA minimum)

## Platform-Specific Image Specs

### Instagram Feed Post (1:1 Square)
- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1080 px
- **Style:** Product shot, lifestyle, or text-based graphic
- **Text overlay:** Optional; if used, keep to upper third or lower third

### Instagram Reel Cover (9:16 Vertical)
- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 px
- **Style:** Eye-catching, text-forward (viewers see this in grid view)
- **Text overlay:** Large, bold headline + small subhead or emoji

### Instagram Carousel Slides (1:1 Square)
- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1080 px per slide
- **Style:** Consistent design across all slides (same background, fonts, colors)
- **Text hierarchy:** One key point per slide; visual simplicity

### Instagram Stories (9:16 Vertical)
- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 px
- **Style:** Casual, playful, ephemeral
- **Text overlay:** Keep text in "safe zone" (center 1/3 vertically; avoid top/bottom 250px)

### YouTube Shorts Thumbnail (9:16 Vertical)
- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 px
- **Style:** Similar to Reel coverâ€”text-forward, bold

### Website/Landing Page Hero Image (16:9 or wider)
- **Dimensions:** 1920 x 1080 px minimum
- **Style:** Lifestyle or product shot with space for text overlay (left or right)

### Facebook/Meta Ads (1:1 or 4:5)
- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1080 px (square) or 1080 x 1350 px (4:5)
- **Style:** Product + benefit text; clear CTA

- [ ] Is the aspect ratio correct for the platform?
- [ ] Does it align with brand voice and visual identity?
