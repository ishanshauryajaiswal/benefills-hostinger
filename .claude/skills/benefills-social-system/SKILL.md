# Benefills Social Media Content System

## Purpose
This Skill provides **repeatable frameworks and templates** for generating on-brand, engagement-optimized social content for Benefills.

**Use when:**
- Creating 7/14/30-day content calendars
- Generating individual posts (captions, scripts, hooks)
- Planning campaigns or themed content weeks
- Optimizing content for specific platforms (IG, YT Shorts, TikTok)

## Platform Strategy Overview

### Instagram (Primary Platform)
- **Feed Posts:** Educational, product-focused, lifestyle, testimonials
- **Reels:** Short-form video (15-60s), trending audio, hooks, quick tips
- **Carousels:** In-depth education (5-10 slides), step-by-step guides
- **Stories:** Daily engagement, polls, Q&A, behind-the-scenes

**Content Pillars:**
1. **Education** (40%) ‚Äî Thyroid health, nutrition, hormones
2. **Product** (30%) ‚Äî Features, benefits, usage ideas, testimonials
3. **Community** (20%) ‚Äî Relatable struggles, encouragement, UGC
4. **Lifestyle** (10%) ‚Äî Routines, recipes, wellness tips

### YouTube Shorts (Secondary)
- 15-60s vertical video
- Focus on quick tips, myths vs. facts, product demos
- Optimize for search: "thyroid-friendly snacks," "PCOS breakfast ideas"

### TikTok (Future Expansion)
- Similar to Reels but more trend-driven, raw, authentic
- Leverage trending sounds and challenges

## Content Review Checklist

Before posting, ask:
- [ ] Does this match our brand voice (warm, credible, empowering)?
- [ ] Is the hook strong enough to stop the scroll?
- [ ] Does this provide value (education, inspiration, or entertainment)?
- [ ] Is there a clear CTA?
- [ ] Are disclaimers included if needed?
- [ ] Are hashtags relevant and strategic?
- [ ] Is this optimized for the platform (aspect ratio, length, format)?

## Monthly Metrics to Track

- **Engagement rate** (likes + comments + shares / followers)
- **Reach** (unique accounts reached)
- **Saves** (indicator of value‚Äîpeople want to reference later)
- **Profile visits** (interest in brand)
- **Link clicks** (traffic to site)
- **Follower growth**

**Goal:** Prioritize saves and comments over vanity metrics (likes). High saves = high value content.
